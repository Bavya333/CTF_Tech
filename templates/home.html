Riddles Of The Sphinx

"The greatest fear known to mankind is the fear of the unknown"

The Riddles of the Sphinx is the online treasure hunt of the Kurukshetra battleground. It has been one of the trademark events of the techno management cultural attracting millions of contestants across the globe. The very fact that this year, K 19 would be hosting the 13th version of the event is a testimony to the magnitude of its popularity. The event embarks you on a labyrinth so intriguing and complex that only the most persistent can come through. A number of puzzles have to be cracked and mysteries unfolded before you reach the final destination to claim your loot.
